Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Cheese

  • Meat Sauce

  • Onion, garlic, carrot, celery
  • Beef
  • Canned tomato and tomato paste
  • Red Wine
  • Seasonings, stock cubes, bay leaves thyme, oregano

  • White Sauce

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Make the white sauce
  3. Assemble: meat, sheet, white, repeat
  4. Cover with cheese
  5. Bake that bad boy
