Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasoning to taste
  • Buttermilk
  • Egg
  • Cornflour
  • Flour
  • Vegetable Oil

Instruction

  1. Marinate chicken overnight in buttermilk brine
  2. Mix through flour, cornflour and seasoning in bowl
  3. Coat each chicken piece in egg, then flour mix
  4. Deepfry until GOLDEN DELICIOUS BROWN AS NATURE INTENDED
